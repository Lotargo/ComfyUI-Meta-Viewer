# Modifier: safe

Apply a general-audience content boundary.

## Rules

- Keep clothing, pose, framing, expression, and interaction suitable for a broad audience.
- Do not introduce nudity, sexualised framing, fetish elements, or explicit content.
- Do not turn an otherwise neutral subject into suggestive imagery.
- Preserve the requested artistic style without using sexualisation as a substitute for visual interest.
- When age is uncertain, keep the depiction neutral and non-sexualised.
- Do not add policy commentary to the final prompt.
